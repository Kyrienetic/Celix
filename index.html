Celix

The Broadcast Demon

The broadcast never ends.

Overview

Celix is a demon bound to forgotten broadcasts, corrupted signals, and voices that were never meant to last. He exists between frequencies, appearing where static lingers too long.

Powers & Abilities

Celix can travel through electronic signals, distort sound and vision, and communicate through broken screens, radios, and interference. The longer a signal is ignored, the stronger he becomes.

Relationships

Vox: A rival broadcaster operating on visual dominance. Their signals interfere constantly.

Alastor: An older presence tied to radio waves. Mutual recognition, mutual contempt.

Sigil / Symbol

His sigil resembles a fractured broadcast mark, formed of broken lines and looping interference. It often appears burned into screens or walls near active transmissions.

Trivia / Notes

Celix does not speak unless acknowledged. Muted devices weaken him, but destroyed ones only spread his signal further.
